## Detailed Competitor Analysis

### LEAD School

**What They Are:**
Full-stack school operating system with curriculum, hardware, and software.

**Strengths:**
- Comprehensive solution
- Trained content and curriculum
- Physical classroom hardware

**Weaknesses:**
- Expensive (Rs 3,000-8,000/student/year estimated)
- School loses autonomy (must use LEAD curriculum)
- Primarily English-focused
- Requires significant infrastructure change

**When Prospects Mention LEAD:**
```
"LEAD is great for schools that want complete transformation.

AI Pathshala is different:
- You keep your curriculum, we add AI tools
- 10x more affordable
- No infrastructure changes needed
- Teachers use it as a tool, not a replacement

We're complementary, not competitive. Some LEAD schools even add AI tools for extra support."
```

### BYJU's

**What They Are:**
B2C learning platform selling directly to students/parents.

**Strengths:**
- High production value content
- Strong brand recognition
- 24/7 learning available

**Weaknesses:**
- Extremely expensive (Rs 20,000-50,000/student/year)
- Bypasses schools entirely
- Schools have no visibility or control
- Sales practices controversial
- Not affordable for APS families

**When Prospects Mention BYJU's:**
```
"BYJU's sells to parents, not schools. They bypass you completely.

AI Pathshala is designed for schools:
- You remain central to education
- Teachers are empowered, not replaced
- You control the student experience
- 100x more affordable per student

Plus, BYJU's families still need school. We make your school better."
```

### Khan Academy / Free Learning Platforms

**What They Are:**
Free online learning content libraries.

**Strengths:**

**Weaknesses:**

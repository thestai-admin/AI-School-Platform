# School Lead Research Guide

## Where to Find Schools

### Primary Sources

#### 1. Google Maps
**Search queries:**
- "private school [city name]"
- "english medium school [city name]"
- "CBSE school [city name]"
- "affordable school [city name]"

**What to capture:**
- School name
- Address
- Phone number
- Website (if available)
- Rating/reviews (quality indicator)

---

#### 2. JustDial
**URL:** justdial.com

**Search:**
- "Private Schools in [City]"
- Filter by area/locality

**What to capture:**
- Owner/Principal name (sometimes listed)
- Direct phone numbers
- Multiple contact numbers

---

#### 3. School Directories

**National:**
- schoolmykids.com
- collegedunia.com/schools
- schooldekho.org
- edustoke.com

**State-specific:**
- Bihar: bseb.gov.in (state board schools)
- UP: upmsp.edu.in
- MP: mponline.gov.in

---

#### 4. Local Yellow Pages
- sulekha.com
- tradeindia.com
- exportersindia.com (yes, they list schools)

---

### Secondary Sources

#### 5. WhatsApp Groups
- Ask current contacts for school owner groups
- Education association groups
- Local business groups

#### 6. LinkedIn
- Search: "School Principal [City]"
- Search: "School Director [City]"
- Look at school pages

#### 7. Facebook
- Search: "[City] schools"
- School Facebook pages often have contact info
- Local parent groups mention schools

## School Qualification Criteria

### Ideal Target (Score 8-10 points)

| Criteria | Points | Check |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Fee range Rs 5,000-50,000/year | 2 | [ ] |
| 200-1000 students | 2 | [ ] |
| Private (not government) | 2 | [ ] |
| Has website or Facebook page | 1 | [ ] |
| Tier 2/3 city | 2 | [ ] |
| Board: CBSE/State (not international) | 1 | [ ] |

**Score 8+:** High priority lead
**Score 5-7:** Medium priority
**Score <5:** Low priority (skip initially)

---

### Red Flags (Skip These)

- [ ] International curriculum (IB, Cambridge) - different needs
- [ ] Fee >Rs 1 lakh/year - will want premium solutions
- [ ] Government school - long procurement cycle
- [ ] <100 students - too small, low revenue potential
- [ ] Just opened (<2 years) - unstable, may not survive
- [ ] No online presence at all - may be too traditional

## Research Process (30 min/day)

### Step 1: Open Google Maps (10 min)
1. Search "private school [target city]"
2. Open 10 schools in new tabs
3. For each, note: Name, phone, rating
4. Skip schools with <3 stars (quality issues)

### Step 2: Cross-reference JustDial (10 min)
1. Search same schools on JustDial
2. Get additional phone numbers
3. Note owner/principal name if available

### Step 3: Quick Website Check (5 min)
1. Visit school website if exists
2. Look for: About page, contact page
3. Note: Fee structure, student count, board

### Step 4: Enter in Tracker (5 min)
1. Add to sales pipeline spreadsheet
2. Score the lead (1-10)
3. Set status: "Lead"

## Lead Enrichment Techniques

### Finding Principal Names

1. **School Website:** Check "About Us" or "Principal's Message"
2. **Google Search:** "[School Name] principal"
3. **LinkedIn:** Search school name, find employees
4. **Call and Ask:** "Principal ji ka naam please?"

### Finding Owner Names (for small schools)

1. **JustDial:** Sometimes lists owner
2. **Registration Records:** ROC if it's a company
3. **Local Knowledge:** Ask other school owners
4. **Direct Ask:** "Owner/Manager se baat karni hai"

### Verifying Phone Numbers

1. **Call during school hours:** 9 AM - 1 PM, 3 PM - 5 PM
2. **WhatsApp:** Send message, see if business account
3. **Truecaller:** Verify name associated with number
